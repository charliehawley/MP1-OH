about

I am a nonfiction filmmaker and freelancer in documentary film. I have worked as a researcher, editor, cameraperson and assistant producer across feature films, shorts, television and charities. Experience includes work with Dartmouth Films, RSA Films, Channel 4, ITV, BBC and Save the Children.


Beyond film production, I have also worked as Production Manager for Open City Documentary Festival, co-facilitated participatory visual storytelling workshops with Yarrow Films and been selected to participate in the HOME Artist Film Lab.


I am currently completing an MA in Visual Anthropology at the University of Manchester. My thesis and accompanying short documentary, situated in a community garden, examines how engagement with the natural and nonhuman aids perspectives on existence, ageing and mortality.
