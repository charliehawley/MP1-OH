about

Olivia is a nonfiction filmmaker who produces sensitive, sensuous studies concerned with human nature and informed by methodologies of visual anthropology.


In her freelance career, Olivia has worked as a researcher, editor, cameraperson and assistant producer across feature films, shorts, television and charity films. Experience includes work with Dartmouth Films, RSA Films, Whalebone Films, BBC, Channel 4, ITV, Channel 5 and Save the Children.


Beyond film production, Olivia has also worked as Production Manager for Open City Documentary Festival (UCL), co-facilitated participatory visual storytelling workshops with Yarrow Films and been selected to participate in the HOME Artist Film Lab.
