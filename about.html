about me

Freelancing in documentary and factual film production, I am drawn to human-interest stories that are working to foster empathy and understanding towards how we treat and perceive each other.


Since graduating in Film Studies from King's College London, I have worked across research, fact checking, securing contributors involving sensitive subject matters, self-shooting, editing, applying for festivals and funding opportunities, working at film festivals (production manager, pre-selector, venue coordinator, marketing assistant) and writing documentary film criticism.


Self-shooting kit (sony a7s), editing software (Premiere Pro) and full clean driving license.
